Snake River in Idaho

Snake! Snake! Snake!!! breathtaking Experience!

There are over 3000 miles of whitewater in Idaho, flowing from high peaks to low valleys through some of the most pristine lands in America. From the densely forested banks of the Salmon River to the high desert canyons of the Snake River, Idaho boasts some of the most thrilling whitewater adventures in the United States. The state encompasses large tracts of roadless wilderness where the only means of discovering landscapes is on foot, horseback or with a paddle in hand. Come face-to-face with bighorn sheep, gaze on black bears and soaring eagles, discover the region’s rich Native American history, and soak in idyllic hot springs before camping beneath brilliantly starlit skies. At our riverside camps, you’ll be treated to delicious cuisine and camaraderie around the campfire during an adventure you’ll never forget.
